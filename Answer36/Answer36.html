36 - Find the largest of two number.

Description: Function `findLargest()` finds the largest between two number by using “>” and “=” operator in JavaScript..

Example:


findLargest(21,45); //"45 the largest number"
findLargest(34,18); //"34 is the largest number"
findLargest(41,41); //"41 is equal to 41" />
